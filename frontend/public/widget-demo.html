Optional settings

Tweak the launcher with data- attributes on the same tag:

  • data-yopey-position="left" — bubble on the left
  • data-yopey-label="Volunteer with us" — launcher text
  • data-yopey-color="#FFAD00" — brand colour
  • data-yopey-entry="/chat" — let returning users resume instead of re-onboarding

Heads up: some browsers (Safari especially) restrict storage for content embedded on another site, so a returning visitor may be asked to onboard again. The full flow — onboard, search, draft an email — always works within a single visit.
